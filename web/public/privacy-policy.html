VitaNova Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell personal health data on external servers. All processing happens locally.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Biometric data: Heart rate, HRV metrics, sleep patterns, step count, GPS routes during workouts, cognitive test scores — all captured from your phone's built-in sensors.
  • Profile data: Name, age, weight, height, fitness goals — entered during onboarding.
  • Usage data: Screen time statistics (with your explicit permission).
  • Mood entries: Self-reported mood selections.

2. How We Store Data

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted Room database. No data is transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share it.

3. Data Sharing

Through the Privacy Matrix feature, you control exactly what data is shared and with whom:

  • Physician — vital signs, sleep data
  • Psychologist — mood entries, stress levels
  • Trainer — fitness metrics, readiness scores
  • Commander — operational readiness only

You can revoke access at any time. No data is shared without your explicit opt-in consent.

4. Third-Party Services

VitaNova does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers. The only third-party integration is Health Connect API (Android), which syncs with your existing health apps (Samsung Health, Google Fit) at your request.

5. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data. There are no cloud backups unless you manually export your data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: View all your data in the app at any time.
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats.
  • Deletion: Delete all data from Settings, or uninstall the app.
  • Rectification: Edit your profile and correct any data.
  • Restriction: Use the Privacy Matrix to restrict data sharing.

7. Children's Privacy

VitaNova is not intended for use by individuals under 16 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes through in-app notifications.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@vitanova.app
